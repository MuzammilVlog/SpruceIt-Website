SPRUCE IT UP

Calgary s Favourite Garden Centre

Spruce It Up is Calgary’s very own full service Garden Centre, open all year-round to help Calgarians during any season!
We have an extensive selection of plants including indoor houseplants, annuals, perennials, trees and shrubs. As well as everything you need to create and maintain your very own oasis. Just like gardening tools, home and garden décor, to bulk materials, growing supplies, and so much more we got you covered!
We’re also proud to carry the largest pottery selection in the city of Calgary! From indoor to outdoor pots we have a large range in sizes, shapes, designs and colours, there is always a pot for everyone!

Our experienced and knowledgeable team is here to help you build the garden of your dreams with creativity, inspiration, and devotion to gardening!

Located on 20-acres in SW Calgary, we offer everything from tropicals, annuals, perennials, and trees & shrubs in our 15,000 sq ft greenhouse, to pottery, giftware and home décor from our 4,000 square foot retail showroom. Our experienced, expert team is also proud to offer full-service landscaping, pruning and fertilizing services to Calgary and the surrounding communities.
